Our Family & Mission

Our Story

Christina & Marc Alfano

Christina and Marc Alfano have been serving the Lord together for over 20 years in both ministry and business. Their journey has taken them across the globe — from the United States to Europe, Latin America, and beyond — carrying the uncompromising message of the Gospel.

With a deep conviction that the Kingdom of God belongs in every area of life, the Alfanos have dedicated themselves to equipping believers to bring transformation in their workplaces, communities, and nations. They are the founders of Marketplace Revival, a movement that empowers everyday believers to take the Gospel beyond the four walls of the church.

Marc and Christina live with a simple vision: to see the Kingdom of God advance in every sphere of influence — business, education, media, government, family, arts, and the church.

Our Journey

20+ Years of Faithful Ministry

From local outreach to global impact, God has been faithful every step of the way.

What began as a calling to serve in their local community has grown into a worldwide ministry spanning more than 19 countries. Through crusades, revival meetings, leadership training, media production, and marketplace equipping, the Alfanos have seen God move powerfully across cultures and continents.

With over two decades of experience in both business and ministry, Marc and Christina bring a unique perspective that bridges the gap between the sacred and the secular — believing there is no divide when it comes to the Kingdom.

Their Marketplace Revival resources reach believers across the globe with practical, Kingdom-focused teaching for everyday life.
