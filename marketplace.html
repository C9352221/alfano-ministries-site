The Movement

What Is Marketplace Revival?

Marketplace Revival is a Kingdom movement that equips believers to take the power and message of the Gospel beyond the four walls of the church and into the marketplace — where they work, live, and lead.

Founded by Marc and Christina Alfano, this initiative provides teaching, resources, and activation for believers who want to see transformation in their workplaces, communities, and nations. It's time for the Church to arise in every area of society.
