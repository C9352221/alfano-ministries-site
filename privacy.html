Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Alfano Ministries through our website, contact form, or mailing list, we may collect the following information: your name, email address, phone number, and any message content you provide. We do not collect information from children under 13.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, send ministry updates and newsletters (if you subscribe), process donations, and improve our website. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Donations & Payment Processing

Donations are processed through third-party platforms such as Zeffy, Stripe, Cash App, Zelle, and PayPal. Alfano Ministries does not store credit card numbers or banking information. Please refer to each platform's privacy policy for details on how they handle your payment data.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality such as remembering your preferences. We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. These tools collect anonymous data and do not identify you personally.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services including YouTube, Spotify, Facebook, and Instagram. These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Email Communications

If you subscribe to our mailing list, you may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly. We respect your inbox and will only send content related to ministry updates, events, and encouragement.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make such a request, please contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
